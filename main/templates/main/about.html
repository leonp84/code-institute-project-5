About Heritage Company

“Tradition is not the worship of ashes,

Timepieces are more than just accessories, they're heirlooms. Luxury watches are luxurious not just because of the obvious - that they are crafted with precision and present the best amplification of crafstmanship a timepiece artisan has to offer, but also because they are designed to accompany you through life’s milestones, from the first promotion to the birth of your child, symbolizing your journey of success and growth. As you start your family and build your future, consider one of our timepieces not only as a statement of your achievements but as a gift of heritage to your children. It’s a way to share your story, a tangible piece of your hard work and dedication that they can cherish forever.


Imagine the day when your child is ready to step into their own future, wearing the watch you once chose with care. It’s a bond that transcends time, a connection between past, present, and future. At Heritage Company, we are dedicated to helping you create these unforgettable moments, ensuring that your legacy is beautifully preserved.

Explore our collection, and find the perfect watch that speaks to your personal journey and aspirations. Start your legacy today with Heritage Company, and let your story be one that echoes through the ages. Each family has its own traditions and purchasing an expensive timepiece is, in many ways, the start of a new tradition. A passing of the fire... as Gustav Mahler so eloquently put it.


Heritage Company was founded in 1984, inspired by a deeply personal moment. The founder received his first luxury timepiece from his father, a cherished inheritance that symbolized more than just the passage of time. This heirloom, passed down through generations, sparked a vision in him to create a brand that celebrated such legacies.

Driven by the desire to craft timepieces that would become treasured family heirlooms, Heritage Company was established. Each watch is given the same attention to detail and timeless elegance that his father's gift embodied, ensuring that every piece becomes a lasting legacy for its owner.
